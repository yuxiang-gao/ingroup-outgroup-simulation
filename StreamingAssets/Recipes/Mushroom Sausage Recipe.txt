Mushroom Sausage Pizza Recipe:

Ingredients/Tools
Pizza Dough
Rolling Pin
Tomato Sauce
Cheese
Mushroom
Sausage

Instructions
1. Place the pizza dough on top of the pizza stone.
2. Use the rolling pin to roll out the dough.
3. Spread tomato sauce onto the pizza.
4. Sprinkle cheese on top.
5. Place mushrooms on the pizza.
6. Place sausage on the pizza.
7. Put the pizza into the oven.
